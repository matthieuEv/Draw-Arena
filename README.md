## Dashboard

When you log in, you will be redirected to your dashboard.

Here, you can see an overview of your activities on the platform: Your club, participations, score, etc...

You can also see Competitions your club is participating in and your recent drawings.

If you are an admin, you will see some statistics about the platform: number of users, clubs, competitions, etc...

## Clubs

In the club section, you can find all the clubs registered on the platform. When you click on a club, you can see its details and all their members.

You can also view your own club details and members by clicking on "My Club".

## Competitions

When you go to the competitions section, you can see all the competitions available on the platform.

You can filter competitions by year.

You can view all the participants of a competition by clicking on "Load More Members".

You can also click on "See the competition" to view more details about the competition. And if the competition is finished, you can see the results.

If you are part of a Competition, you can "Drop a Drawing" by clicking on the button and uploading your drawing file. After uploading, you have to fill the CAPTCHA to validate your submission.

## Drawings

In the drawings section, you can see all the drawings submitted on the platform. You can filter drawings by date.

## Statistics

Here, you can see some statistics about the platform: number of clubs, users, evaluations.

You can also see some intresting graphs with more detailed statistics.
